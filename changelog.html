v1.39.50 2026-07-03 最新Latest
  • 新增交易诊断 —— 统计页新增「交易诊断」:先确定性深挖你的成交,再由 AI 分章写成诊断报告;满 30 笔可生成。
  • 新增复诊与诊断历史 —— 复诊自动和上一次报告对比,历次诊断留档、随时回看。
  • 新增回测逐笔回放 —— 回测结果能像账单复盘一样整段播放、可调倍速。
  • 新增回放「全程战绩」面板 —— 期望值、盈亏比、胜率、盈亏构成一眼看全。
  • 优化回放体验多处 —— 进入秒开、「看全部」即时切换、布林 / KDJ 策略回放自动挂指标、统计概览曲线改画累计净盈亏。
  • 修复回放长时间播放的周期性卡顿,以及退出回放、看全部等一批问题。
  • NewTrade Diagnosis — a new Statistics tab that digs through your trades deterministically, then has AI write a chaptered diagnosis report; available from 30 trades.
  • NewFollow-up diagnosis & history — re-diagnose and it auto-compares with your last report; every report is archived for review.
  • NewTrade-by-trade backtest replay — play a backtest through like a statement review, with adjustable speed.
  • NewRun-stats panel for replays — expectancy, payoff ratio, win rate and P&L breakdown at a glance.
  • ImprovedReplay polish — instant open, instant “Fit all”, Bollinger / Stochastic runs auto-attach their indicators, and the overview equity curve now plots cumulative net P&L.
  • FixedThe periodic stall during long playback, plus a batch of replay exit / “Fit all” issues.

  • FixedConsistent P&L accounting — Closed a case where commission was double-counted in some views (inflating profit); P&L now reconciles everywhere.
  • FixedSteadier replay — Manual sessions play through to the live data edge and no longer stall at an old boundary after a data update; balance recompute on rewind adds back deposits/withdrawals; swap is persisted on close and no longer zeroed.
  • FixedChart templates & misc — Applying a template affects only the current chart; futures SL/TP labels show in the account's currency (¥); BTCJPY / ETHJPY are no longer misclassified as JPY FX; subplot zoom no longer clips bars.
